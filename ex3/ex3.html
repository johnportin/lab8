Biography

Hi there! My name is Tobie. I was born into a litter of five. My mom thought I was weak because I was small. I guess they called me the "runt."

One day, a some strange humans came to take me away from my mom. They were nice to me. They fed, bathed and pet me. I spent thirteen years living with them.

Behind our house was a park. The family - mostly my new mom and dad - would take me on walks there daily. Occassionally I got to go to a facility where there were many other dogs. My parents dropped me there in the morning, and picked me up at night. I had time to play with the other dogs, and made many friends.

My two favorite activities were chasing squirrels and chasing humans. I loved standing in the back door protecting the yard. When I spot a squirrel, I immediately let out a terrifying pig squeal, and chase that vermin out of the yard. Inside, my family has a round table. We love to run in circles, occasionally changing direction suddenly! I'm not sure exactly what the game is, but I know that I always win.

Near the end of my life, I developed liver cancer. I started to slow down. Jumping on the couch became hard, and long walks were tiresome. My family continued to care for me during this time. They made sure I was comfortable. Since I wasn't digesting food very well, they added chicken and eggs to my food for some extra flavor and calories.

On April 15th, 2021 I made my peace with the world. I went with my family to the vet, and I never left.
